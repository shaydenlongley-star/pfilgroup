Why Join Us

Why Join PFIL Group?

Career Growth

We invest in our people's development. With four growing business divisions, there are real pathways to advance your career within the Group.

Great Culture

We're a Pacific company with Pacific values — we look after our own, celebrate success together, and support each other through challenges.

Learning & Development

Diverse Opportunities

Local Employer

Our Workplace
Where You'll Work

Our Workplace

We're proud of the workplaces we've built at PFIL Group. Whether you're working on the floor of PTH Tonga, coordinating freight at Dateline Shipping, or driving broadband rollout at WanTok Network — you'll find a supportive, professional environment where Pacific people thrive.

We are committed to health, safety, and the wellbeing of every person in our team. Our workplaces are safe, inclusive, and designed to help our people perform at their best.

We celebrate the diversity of our team and work hard to ensure that everyone — regardless of background, gender, or experience level — has equal opportunity to succeed and grow within the Group.

Application Process

How to Apply

Our application process is straightforward. Here's what to expect when you apply to join the PFIL Group.

1

Browse & Apply

Find a role that interests you above, then submit your CV and a brief cover letter via our contact page or by emailing us directly.

2

Initial Review

Our team will review your application and reach out within a few business days if your experience matches what we're looking for.

3

Interview

Shortlisted candidates are invited for an interview — either in person or via video call — to discuss the role, your experience, and your fit with the team.

4

Offer & Onboarding

Successful candidates receive a formal offer and are guided through our onboarding process to make sure you start your PFIL journey on the right foot.
