Community Outreach

Business success means nothing without community wellbeing. PFIL Group is committed to giving back to the Pacific communities that make us who we are.

Community Sponsorship
Our Commitment

More Than Business — A Commitment to Community

At PFIL Group, our tagline "Building A Better Tonga" is more than marketing — it's a genuine commitment to the communities we operate in and the people who make up those communities.

We believe sustainable businesses have a responsibility to go beyond profit. Every year, PFIL Group and its subsidiaries invest in social programmes, educational initiatives, environmental efforts, and community development projects across Tonga and Vanuatu.

We are proud to employ hundreds of Pacific people, support local suppliers and contractors, and contribute to the economic fabric of the nations where we operate.

How We Give Back

Our Programmes

From education to environment, our community programmes touch every aspect of life in the Pacific communities we serve.

Education & Scholarships

We invest in the next generation of Pacific talent through scholarships, school sponsorships and vocational training partnerships.

Community Infrastructure

PFIL Group supports the development and maintenance of community facilities — from sports grounds and halls to public spaces.

Youth Sport & Recreation

We sponsor local sporting clubs, youth teams, and recreational programmes that keep Pacific communities active and connected.

Environmental Stewardship

We support reef, ocean and land conservation initiatives, and work to minimise the environmental footprint of our operations.

Food Security & Relief

When natural disasters or hardship strikes, PFIL Group actively supports food relief programmes and emergency response initiatives.

Local Employment

Committed to hiring locally, our businesses provide meaningful employment and career development to hundreds of Pacific people.

Community Story

Sponsorship of the Takuilau College Sports Team

PTH Tonga Ltd & Bin-Go – a sub of Dateline Transam Ltd combined sponsorship of the Takuilau College Sports team, that has awarded us with huge local and international (international Tongan communities) coverage as the sports team produced 12 gold medals in total with silvers & bronze.

Miss. Lusia Loni of the Junior grade was the hailed sports girl of the year for the junior grade, having won gold for all her 5 events of 100mtrs, 200mtrs, 400mtrs, 800mtrs, and the 1500mtrs.

The Pacific Way

The Spirit of Pacific Community

The Pacific Islands have a tradition of community, of mutual support, and of looking after one another. These values are woven into Pacific culture — and they are woven into the way PFIL Group operates.

We don't see community investment as charity. We see it as a natural expression of who we are and where we come from. We are members of these communities, not just guests in them.
