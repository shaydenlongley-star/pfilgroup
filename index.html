Who We Are

A Pacific Holding Company With Purpose

Pacific Finance & Investment Ltd. (PFIL) is a diversified holding company headquartered in Nukuʻalofa, Kingdom of Tonga. We invest in and manage businesses that serve communities across Tonga and Vanuatu.

Our portfolio spans retail hardware & timber, international shipping logistics, and telecommunications — providing the essential goods, services and connectivity that Pacific communities depend on every day.

Our Story
Our Story
"We invest in the Pacific because we believe in the Pacific — its people, its potential, and its future."

From a single hardware store in Nukuʻalofa to a diversified group spanning retail, logistics, and telecommunications — every step has been guided by one conviction: that thoughtful investment builds better communities.
