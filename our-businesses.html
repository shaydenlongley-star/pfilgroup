Tonga's #1 Hardware Retailer
PTH Tonga Ltd.

Pacific Timber & Hardware — Tonga

PTH Tonga Ltd. is the Kingdom of Tonga's leading retailer of timber, hardware, building materials, and tools. For years, we have been the trusted partner of homeowners, tradespeople, and commercial builders throughout the Kingdom.

Our wide-ranging product selection covers everything from structural timber and roofing iron to power tools, plumbing fittings, paint, and general hardware. Our knowledgeable team helps customers find the right product for every project.

PTH Vanuatu Ltd.

Pacific Timber & Hardware — Vanuatu

Building on the success of PTH Tonga, PFIL Group extended the Pacific Timber & Hardware brand to Vanuatu, bringing the same commitment to quality, service and product range to a new Pacific market.

PTH Vanuatu serves the hardware and building materials needs of the Vanuatu market, offering a comprehensive range of products to tradespeople, builders and homeowners across the island nation.

Dateline Shipping Tonga

Reliable Pacific Freight & Logistics

Dateline Shipping Tonga provides reliable freight and logistics services connecting Tonga with global supply chains. For businesses and individuals, Dateline Shipping is the trusted name for importing and exporting goods.

We handle everything from full container loads to smaller freight consignments, offering door-to-door solutions that simplify international trade. Our experienced team navigates customs, documentation and delivery so you don't have to.

Connecting the Pacific
WanTok Network

Pacific Connectivity & Telecommunications

WanTok Network is PFIL's telecommunications division, delivering broadband internet and connectivity across the Pacific. Following its strategic acquisition of Telsat Broadband, WanTok Network is rapidly growing its reach.

WanTok Network is on a mission to bridge the digital divide — providing affordable, reliable internet access to homes, businesses, schools and communities throughout the Pacific.
