Tonga's #1 Hardware Retailer
PTH Tonga Ltd.

Pacific Timber & Hardware — Tonga

PTH Tonga Ltd. is the Kingdom of Tonga's leading wholesale and retail business for timber, hardware, building materials, and tools. For years, we have been the trusted partner of homeowners, tradespeople, and commercial builders throughout the Kingdom.

Our wide-ranging product selection covers everything from structural timber and roofing iron to power tools, plumbing fittings, paint, and general hardware. Our knowledgeable team helps customers find the right product for every project.

Hardware & Retail — Vanuatu
PTH Vanuatu Ltd.

Pacific Timber & Hardware — Vanuatu

Building on the success of PTH Tonga, PFIL Group extended the Pacific Timber & Hardware brand to Vanuatu, bringing the same commitment to quality, service and product range to a new Pacific market.

In 2015, Pacific Retail Limited acquired Alubat Limited in Vanuatu — an aluminium joinery company — using it as a platform to establish PTH Vanuatu and serve the hardware and building materials needs of the Vanuatu market.

Dateline TransAM Ltd. (DTL)

Reliable Pacific Freight & Logistics

Dateline TransAM Ltd. (DTL) provides reliable freight and logistics services connecting Tonga with global supply chains. For businesses and individuals, DTL is the trusted name for importing and exporting goods throughout the Pacific.

We handle everything from full container loads to smaller freight consignments, offering door-to-door solutions that simplify international trade. Our experienced team navigates customs, documentation and delivery so you don't have to.

Broadband — Tonga
TokoWireless Tonga — WanTok Tonga

TokoWireless Tonga & WanTok Tonga

TokoWireless Tonga, operating under the WanTok Tonga brand, is PFIL Group's telecommunications arm serving the Kingdom of Tonga. Following the strategic acquisition of Telsat Broadband, WanTok Tonga is rapidly expanding broadband coverage across the Kingdom.

WanTok Tonga is committed to making the internet accessible and affordable for every Tongan — whether at home, at school, or running a business. Reliable Pacific connectivity starts here.
