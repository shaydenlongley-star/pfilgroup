Who We Are

A Pacific holding company with a clear purpose — building a better future for the Kingdom of Tonga and the broader Pacific region.

Our Story

Built for the Pacific, by the Pacific

Pacific Finance & Investment Ltd. (PFIL) was established with one driving conviction: that thoughtful, well-managed investment in the Pacific could deliver lasting economic and social benefits to the communities that need it most.

Headquartered in Nukuʻalofa, Kingdom of Tonga, PFIL has grown into a diversified holding company with operations across Tonga and Vanuatu. Our portfolio spans retail hardware and timber, international shipping, and telecommunications.

We are a Pacific company through and through. Our leadership, our workforce, and our values are all deeply rooted in the culture and spirit of the islands we call home.

Our Mission

To invest in and manage a portfolio of Pacific businesses that deliver real value to customers, meaningful employment to communities, and sustainable returns to the Group — all in service of a stronger, more prosperous Pacific.

Our Vision

To be the Pacific's most trusted and respected investment group — recognised for the quality of our businesses, the depth of our community engagement, and the positive impact we make on the lives of Pacific people.

Our History

Our Journey

Key milestones in the growth of Pacific Finance & Investment Ltd.

Foundation

PFIL Established

Pacific Finance & Investment Ltd. is founded in Nukuʻalofa, Kingdom of Tonga, with a vision to create a diversified Pacific investment group.

Hardware & Retail

PTH Tonga Ltd. Launched

Pacific Timber & Hardware opens in Tonga, quickly establishing itself as the Kingdom's leading hardware and building materials retailer.

Logistics

Dateline Shipping Tonga

PFIL expands into freight and logistics with Dateline Shipping Tonga, providing essential import and export services for the Kingdom.

Regional Expansion

PTH Vanuatu Ltd. Opens

The PTH brand extends beyond Tonga as PFIL establishes Pacific Timber & Hardware operations in Vanuatu.

March 2021

WanTok Acquires Telsat Broadband

PFIL's telecommunications arm WanTok Network announces its acquisition of Telsat Broadband, entering Pacific connectivity services.

Today & Beyond

Continuing to Build a Better Tonga

PFIL continues to grow its portfolio, invest in its people, and deepen its commitment to communities across the Pacific.
